# Code of Conduct

This project is a small, friendly open-source effort. A few simple rules:

- **Be respectful.** Disagree with ideas, not people. No harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- **Be constructive.** Bug reports, feature requests, and criticism are all welcome — bring enough detail that someone can act on it.
- **Assume good intent.** Most misunderstandings are just that.

Unacceptable behavior can be reported privately to **teig.stephan@gmail.com**. The maintainer may edit, lock, or remove contributions and block contributors who violate these rules.
